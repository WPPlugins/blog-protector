== Description ==

Protect your valuable blog content(Pages & Posts) as well as images from getting copied.
This Plugin Can Prevent Users From Copying Content from your blog.

= Features =
* **Disable right click** on your blog 
* **Disable selection of text** on your blog
* **Disable dragging of images** on your blog so that images can't be saved using.
* **Disable Microsoft Image Toolbar** to protect images from your blog.

You must enable the features from options page to protect your blog.


By : Anurag  (http://www.pcdrome.com/).

== Installation ==

1. Upload `blog-protector.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to **Settings** and then **Blog Protector** to configure and protect your blog.
